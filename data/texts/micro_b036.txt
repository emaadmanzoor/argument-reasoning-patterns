Should video games be made olympic?

a1: Video games ought to be included in the ranks of Olympic events.
a2: The performance often required by players is considerable and by all means comparable to physical efforts.
a3: Various skills are promoted and required by different games,
a4: including planning abilities, reaction time, and reasoning to name but a few.
a5: Considering their requirements, some video games are not relevant as sports,
a6: but this issue should be solvable through a clearly structured preselection process.
