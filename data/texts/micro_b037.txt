Should the the morning-after pill be sold over the counter at the pharmacy?

a1: The morning-after pill ought to be available over-the-counter in pharmacies.
a2: Even with state-of-the-art contraceptives there can be mistakes and problems
a3: and an unwanted pregnancy can place a significant strain on both the parents and the child for the rest of their lives.
a4: Some worry about careless use of such measures,
a5: but this problem should rather be addressed with comprehensive prior education.
