Should there be a cap on rent increases for a change of tenant?

a1: 'Property entails commitment' is one of the tenets of our social order.
a2: Everyone should be able to get the utmost profit from his or her property.
a3: A fixing of rent prices according to the market alone, however, will lead to a further division of society.
a4: The rich and poor live in different neighbourhoods.
a5: A constant increase in rent upon a change of tenants hence cannot be desired by society.
