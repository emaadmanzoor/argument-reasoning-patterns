Should Germany introduce the death penalty?

a1: No human being or human committee should again be given the permission to rule over 'life or death'.
a2: Courts are also subject to human error.
a3: That's why Germany should not introduce capital punishment!
a4: Every human, even those who have committed a despicable crime, can bring themselves to regret and change their opinion.
a5: A door must remain open for making amends.
