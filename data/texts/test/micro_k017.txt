Should the statutory retirement age remain at 63 years in the future?

a1: The implementation of retirement at 63 is no longer socially sustainable,
a2: as the population in Germany has, viewed demographically, a disproportionate number of old people,
a3: and constantly declining birth rates are being recorded.
a4: Admittedly the number of immigrants is constantly rising in Germany,
a5: but without sufficient, well-qualified junior employees there is hardly a possibility for adequate pension financing.
