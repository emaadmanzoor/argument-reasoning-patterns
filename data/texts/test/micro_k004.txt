Should shopping malls generally be allowed to open on holidays and Sundays?

a1: Supermarkets and shopping centres should be allowed to open on any Sundays and holidays.
a2: This may be a change for the employees that cannot to be implemented easily.
a3: However, especially for working customers this would mean greater flexibility and fewer nerve-racking peak times during the week.
a4: Considering the growing digitization of society, the traditional model of the weekend will soon be obsolete.
a5: Many European countries, for instance the Netherlands, Sweden and Great Britain, have already understood this and successfully adjusted the opening hours for retailers.
