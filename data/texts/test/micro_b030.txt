Should school uniforms be worn again in our schools?

a1: School uniforms should not be worn in our schools.
a2: They do offer the benefit that the competition over brand-name clothes and the discrimination of the socially weak are prevented.
a3: Nevertheless hardly anyone wishes to dress themselves or their children in a standard uniform.
a4: Plus such uniforms also stand for ideological conformity and for the suppression of individual self-development.
