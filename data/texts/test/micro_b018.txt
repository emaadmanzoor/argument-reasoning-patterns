Should public health insurance cover treatments in complementary and alternative medicine?

a1: The choice of an attending physician is at the discretion of the patient.
a2: Yet the funds of the health insurance companies are a common benefit for all premium payers.
a3: They should not be spent on personal preferences without proven use. The effectiveness of many natural remedies has not been demonstrated.
a5: Therefore no unproven and unrecognized treatments by naturopaths should be reimbursed by the insurance companies.
