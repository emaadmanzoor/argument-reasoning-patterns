Should intelligence services be regulated more tightly by parliament?

a1: Secret services must be more tightly regulated in order to prevent excessive surveillance.
a2: After all, secret services tend to develop a life of their own (a state within a state), which would be irresponsible for democratic states.
a3: Besides, intelligence services intervene massively in the privacy of citizens
a4: and must therefore be monitored more closely to prevent abuse.
a5: Parliamentary control however hampers the work of intelligence services
a6: for it jeopardizes the secrecy and security of confidential data and informants.
