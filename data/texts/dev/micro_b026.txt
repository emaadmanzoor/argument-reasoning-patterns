Should public health insurance cover treatments in complementary and alternative medicine?

a1: The statutory health insurance companies should also cover treatments with non-medical and alternative practitioners.
a2: Their treatment methods and successes are not amply researched or proven.
a3: Yet many patients subjectively experience relief or are cured of their complaints,
a4: which ultimately also saves costs for conventional treatments and medication.
a5: Also conventional medicine is in many cases not successful,
a6: and, moreover, entails significant risks and side effects.
