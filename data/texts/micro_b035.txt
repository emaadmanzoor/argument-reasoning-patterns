Should public health insurance cover treatments in complementary and alternative medicine?

a1: Complementary medicine includes treatments with proven health benefits in many areas
a2: which are often less costly than medication from conventional medicine, which are also paid for after all.
a3: Often natural treatments do not put as much of a strain on the patient.
a4: Such therapies can frequently result in an improvement in the condition through the active participation of the patient.
a5: The health insurance companies ought to be enabled to recognize a doctor's decision for natural treatment and also to cover it.
