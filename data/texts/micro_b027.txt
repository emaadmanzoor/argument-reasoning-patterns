Should Germany introduce the death penalty?

a1: The death penalty should be abandoned everywhere.
a2: From a Christian and humanistic perspective it is not allowed to kill someone.
a3: Proponents of the death penalty count on its deterring effect as well as the ultimate elimination of any potential threat.
a4: However also the threat of the death penalty in the respective countries apparently cannot prevent murder and violent crime.
a5: Moreover it turns out time and again that innocent people are also convicted and executed.
