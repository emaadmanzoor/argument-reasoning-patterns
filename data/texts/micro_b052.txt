Should the statutory retirement age remain at 63 years in the future?

a1: Although the workload is getting heavier in many professions
a2: and physical and psychological wear on personnel is steadily increasing,
a3: the statutory retirement age should in the future not remain at 63 years.
a4: For, due to the low birth rate, the proportion of the elderly population and with it pension costs are on the rise,
a5: and furthermore, due to today's healthcare most people in many occupations are fitter for work in old age than before.
